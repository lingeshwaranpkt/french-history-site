The Students Revolts of May 1968

Introduction

The May 1968 student revolts in France were a series of protests and strikes that began with students demonstrating against the educational system but quickly expanded to include workers, creating a massive wave of unrest across the country.

The Protests

Students occupied universities, and workers staged strikes, leading to major disruptions throughout France. The revolt forced the government of President Charles de Gaulle to address the demands for social change, labor rights, and democratic reforms.
