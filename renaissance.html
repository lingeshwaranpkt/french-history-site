The Renaissance & Humanism in France

Introduction

The Renaissance, beginning in the 14th century, was a period of cultural rebirth in Europe. France, as a center of artistic and intellectual activity, saw the rise of Humanism, which emphasized the value of individualism and classical learning.

Impact of the Renaissance

Under kings like Francis I, France became a hub for art, literature, and learning. The influence of Humanism encouraged the exploration of new ideas, making significant contributions to art, architecture, and philosophy.
