France During World War 1

Introduction

World War 1 (1914-1918) was a devastating global conflict that involved France as one of the Allied Powers. The war had a profound impact on France, with significant loss of life and a devastated landscape.

French Involvement

France was a central player in the Western Front of the war. The Battle of the Somme and the Battle of Verdun were some of the most notable and brutal conflicts where French soldiers fought against German forces.
