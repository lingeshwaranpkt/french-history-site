France During World War II

Introduction

France was heavily involved in World War II, experiencing occupation by Nazi Germany and collaboration with the Vichy regime. The country was divided, with the northern regions under German control and the southern parts governed by the Vichy government.

Occupation and Resistance

The French Resistance played a crucial role in sabotaging German operations. Iconic figures like Charles de Gaulle emerged, calling for French unity and the liberation of their homeland.
