Napoleon & The Battle of Waterloo

Introduction

Napoleon Bonaparte was one of history's most influential military leaders. His rule, however, came to a dramatic end at the Battle of Waterloo in 1815, marking the conclusion of the Napoleonic Wars.

The Battle of Waterloo

The Battle of Waterloo was fought between Napoleon's French forces and the allied armies of the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, and Prussia. It was a crushing defeat for Napoleon, leading to his abdication and exile to Saint Helena.
